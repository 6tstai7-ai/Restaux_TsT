# Pilot Meeting Script — Restaux MVP Validation

**Goal:** Validate pain points, price point ($500/month), and collect initial data for Sprint 1.
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Context:** Independent Restaurant (Quebec), Bilingual (FR/EN)

---

## 1. Introduction (5 min)
- **The Vision:** "Transforming your stock data into revenue."
- **The Hook:** "We noticed independent restaurants often lose 10-15% of their margin to food waste or slow-moving inventory. Restaux is built to detect that surplus via AI and turn it into instant promotions sent to your customers."
- **Pilot Goal:** "We want to use your restaurant as our flagship pilot to prove we can reduce your waste by 20% in 90 days."

## 2. Pain Point Validation (15 min)
*Objective: Listen more than talk.*
- **Inventory:** "How do you track what comes in today? Pen and paper? Excel? Nothing?"
- **Waste:** "What happens when you have too much of a specific ingredient (e.g., 20kg of salmon that expires in 48h)? How do you decide on a 'special'?"
- **Loyalty:** "How do you track your regulars? Do you have their phone numbers or emails?"
- **Promotion:** "If you have a surplus today, how do you tell your customers *right now*?"

## 3. Conceptual Walkthrough (10 min)
*Describe the flow (no code needed, or show the .pen design if ready).*
1. **Receiving:** You scan the barcode of the box/invoice.
2. **AI Analysis:** Restaux sees you have a surplus of [Ingredient] and suggests: *"Send 15% discount on [Dish] via SMS to 450 loyalty members."*
3. **Action:** You click "Approve".
4. **Result:** Customers receive a notification on their Apple/Google Wallet card.

## 4. Data Collection (15 min)
*Ask for access to these for Sprint 1 development:*
- **Ingredient List:** Can we get your top 50 ingredients list (Excel/PDF)?
- **Supplier Invoices:** Can we see 2-3 recent invoices to see the barcode/format?
- **Current CRM:** Do you have a list of emails/phones of regulars?
- **Apple/Google Wallet:** "Would your customers use a digital loyalty card?"

## 5. Pricing & Commitment (10 min)
- **The Offer:** "For the pilot, the setup ($800) is waived. The monthly is $500 CAD."
- **Validation:** "Does $500/month feel like an investment that pays for itself if we reduce waste by 20%?"
- **Next Step:** "We start Sprint 1 on Monday. Can we schedule a 15-min check-in same time next week?"

## 6. Closing (5 min)
- Summary of action items.
- Thank you.

---

## Post-Meeting Checklist for Founder
- [ ] Confirm price was accepted without hesitation (or if they pushed back).
- [ ] List the top 3 ingredients they struggle with.
- [ ] Note their current "manual" way of doing promos.
- [ ] Confirm if they prefer SMS or Email for their clientele.
