# Product

Project name: Restaux

Goal:
Build a French-first SaaS MVP for independent Quebec restaurants. The pilot restaurant is La Boite Jaune. The product turns surplus food into revenue by asking the chef what needs to move, generating a promotion with AI, sending it to opted-in customers, and reinforcing loyalty through Apple/Google Wallet-style points.

Target users:
- Restaurant owner or manager.
- Counter staff recording visits and redemptions.
- Customers joining the loyalty base from a QR/public form.

Core MVP features:
- Owner login.
- One restaurant profile for the pilot.
- CRM customers with SMS consent traceability.
- Public enrollment form.
- Weekly/free-text audit workflow.
- AI-generated promotion copy in French.
- SMS sending to opted-in customers.
- Wallet pass generation.
- Visit registration and point accrual.
- Manual point redemption.

Business goal:
Deliver a demo-ready pilot by the deadline in `CLAUDE.md`, focused only on the critical path that proves value to La Boite Jaune.

Out of scope unless explicitly approved:
- Bilingual UI or i18n.
- Stripe or automated billing.
- Inventory modeling.
- POS integrations.
- Multi-location.
- Blog, marketing site, or customer portal.
- Native mobile app.
- Multi-agent AI orchestration.
